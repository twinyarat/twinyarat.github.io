They span various fields from physics and probability to computer science and mathematics. Many are from the lectures I attended. Some are findings from research. A few are accidental encounters. Most are instrumental in the projects I have worked on. If you happen to find faults in any of them, please do let me know; I would appreciate learning from your correction and criticism.
